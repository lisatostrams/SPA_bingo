Verspreiding

Broedgebied => Palearctic : widespread in subarctic and temperate wet grassland: Iceland, Faroe Is., British Is., Scandinavia s to sw France, e through nw Russia, n Kazakhstan, n Mongolia e to Chukotskiy Peninsula, Kamchatka, Sakhalin and Amurland (e Russia) and Inner Mongolia (ne China)
Overwintering => Africa, Oriental Region, Australasia : coastal and inland temperate to tropical wetlands: s British Is., s Europe, Macaronesia (nw of n Africa; scarce), Africa (except s), Middle East, s, se Asia, Indonesian Archipelago, s New Guinea, Australia; Ryukyu Is. (=Nansei Shoto, s Japan), Taiwan, se China and Philippines

Ondersoorten

L. l. islandica  - Iceland, Faroe Is., Shetland Is. and n Norway | sw Europe
L. l. limosa  - w, c Europe to c Asia | Mediterranean, Africa and India
L. l. melanuroides  - c, e Asia | India, se Asia and Australia
L. l. bohaii   - Russian Far East (presumptively) | ne China, Hong Kong, Vietnam and Malay Peninsula
